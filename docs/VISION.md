# RoomCraft — vision

Where [`STRATEGY.md`](STRATEGY.md) says *what we build right now*, this document says
**where RoomCraft is heading** — the destination the current phase is a step toward.
Its job in the pipeline is to give proposals a direction: prefer work that moves toward
this picture, not just local polish.

> **Status: partly drafted.** The near-term horizon below is derived from the existing
> docs. The longer-term destination is marked **[NEEDS INPUT]** — it isn't stated
> anywhere in the repo yet, and only the product owner can set it. See the questions at
> the end.

## The near-term horizon (derived)

The current phase is unambiguous: **get the core room-planning experience genuinely
good before anything else.** In practice, "we've arrived" for this phase means the three
hard problems in [`TECHNICAL-CHALLENGES.md`](TECHNICAL-CHALLENGES.md) are solved *well*,
not just adequately:

1. A GUI that stays **stupidly simple** on a phone while still exposing every important
   feature.
2. Validation rules that are both **general and relevant** — they fire on real problems
   and never nag about fine layouts.
3. An AI recommendation engine that gives **genuinely best** suggestions for a specific
   room, reliably and fast enough to feel effortless.

Only once that foundation is strong do we turn to how RoomCraft makes money — the
sequence in `STRATEGY.md` is deliberate: **core experience first, monetization later.**
